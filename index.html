Cheesecakes by Jon

I started making cheesecakes a few years ago just for fun, but it turned into a passion when I saw how people enjoyed them. When I had friends and family asking where I bought them from, or ordered them from, I realized I have a talent for something that made a lot of people happy. That's why I created Cheesecakes By Jon.

Our cheesecakes are always fresh, never frozen, and made especially for you. We can accommodate most any order, whether you choose something from our regular menu, or have a special idea in mind. I'm always playing with new ideas, and if you have a special request, don't hesitate to ask. For any cheesecake order, please allow at least 72 hours notice. For orders of 3 or more, please allow 7-10 days notice.
